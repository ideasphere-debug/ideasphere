🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

🌱 Simple version: We don't sell your data. We collect only what we need to run the platform. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Who We Are

IdeaSphere ("we", "us", "our") is a free, open community platform where people from around the world share ideas. We are not a commercial company — we are a community project. You can contact us at: ideasphere.contact@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

When you use IdeaSphere, we may collect:

  • Account information: Your name and email address (via Google Login)
  • Profile photo: Your Google profile picture (optional)
  • Ideas you submit: Title, description, category, and your country
  • Votes and comments: Your interactions on the platform
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent (via Google Analytics — anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your ideas and profile on the platform
  • To send you email notifications about your ideas (via EmailJS)
  • To show community statistics (e.g., "87 countries")
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns

We do NOT use your data for advertising, and we do NOT sell your data to anyone.

4. Google Login (OAuth)

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. When you log in with Google:

  • Google shares your name, email, and profile photo with us
  • We store this in Firebase (Google's secure database)
  • We never see or store your Google password

You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Keeping you logged in (Firebase authentication)
  • Anonymous analytics (Google Analytics)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / Global)

You have the right to:

  • ✅ Access — request a copy of your data
  • ✅ Delete — delete your account and all your data
  • ✅ Correct — update incorrect information
  • ✅ Withdraw consent — stop using the platform anytime

To exercise any of these rights, email us: ideasphere.contact@gmail.com

7. Data Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption. We follow security best practices and regularly review our systems. However, no internet system is 100% secure — use the platform responsibly.

8. Children's Privacy

IdeaSphere is open to students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect additional data from users under 13 beyond what is required for login. Parents can contact us to request data deletion for their child.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify registered users by email when significant changes are made. Continued use of IdeaSphere after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at:

📧 ideasphere.contact@gmail.com

We aim to respond within 48 hours.
